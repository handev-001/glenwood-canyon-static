Resort Near Glenwood Springs

Nestled in a world of its own in the spectacular Glenwood Canyon, the Glenwood Canyon Resort offers our guests a unique experience they simply will not find in any hotel in town. You’ll actually be in the most perfect wilderness setting yet just minutes from Glenwood Springs and all the town has to offer.

Rocky Mountains Camping & Lodging

Imagine yourself arriving through our private entryway facing sky high canyon walls right in front of you. As you continue down our drive you’ll see a multitude of lodging options on either side of you, all with spectacular views of the Glenwood Canyon and the Rocky Mountain “Flat Tops”. We proudly offer a variety of accommodations to fit your lifestyle as well as your budget. From luxury suites and condos perched high above the Colorado River to charming deluxe resort cabins, quaint cottages, cozy riverfront cabins and rustic camper cabins, we’ve got a choice for you and yes, even your four legged family member in designated cabins.

Glenwood Springs Cabin Rentals

Picture yourself relaxing on your own private deck enjoying the sights of the canyon walls and sounds of the Colorado River and nature all around you. You may even catch a glimpse of a deer resting at the river’s edge sipping a cool drink of water, or perhaps a family of bighorn sheep scaling up and down the mountainside, or even an eagle soaring high overhead.

Our cozy efficiency cottages will totally delight the couple or small family looking for a major step up from the ordinary jammed hotel room. Our very popular resort cabins have full kitchens, large decks with barbecues and can accommodate up to 5 guests, even your pet! Our family cabins are the perfect solution for a large family. Our two and three bedroom suites are the ultimate in luxury with amazing views of the river and canyon walls. How about peacefully lounging just inches away from the mighty Colorado River, perhaps preparing your dinner on the campfire, toasting marshmallows or sipping your favorite (highly anticipated) beverage after a full day of exhausting fun in the sun? Yep, that’s a nightly occurrence at one of our riverfront cabins!

Activities On-Site & In Glenwood Springs

Glenwood Canyon Resort has so much more to offer than your typical in-town hotel room. Not only do we have a multitude of lodging options to meet your every need, we also service our guests with a wide range of activities at the Canyon Club, fabulous food & beverage at the No Name Bar and Grill, and the most popular outdoor activities in the area including rafting, kayaking, fishing and hiking! There is just no way to experience all of this, and much more, at your local hotel.
